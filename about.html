About Kenya's Tourism

Our Story

Kenya has been a global tourism destination since the early 1900s. Today, over 2 million visitors arrive annually to witness the world's greatest wildlife spectacles and experience authentic African hospitality.Fun fact Nairobi is the only city in the world with a national park We also have mount Kenya the second highest mountain after Kilimanjaro,which is also snow-capped with three peaks!.Come and witness the great wilderbeest migration in the great Mara river from the Serengeti national park.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable tourism that benefits local communities while preserving Kenya's natural heritage for future generations.
